Maximize Your Teaching Efficiency with Coursistant

A virtual teaching assistant platform that streamlines learning by providing intuitive tools for uploading educational materials and training a virtual assistant for interactive student engagement on Piazza.

Real-Time Piazza Engagement

Automatically monitors and responds to new posts on Piazza in real-time, ensuring swift and proactive student support to enhance learning engagement.

Multimodal Learning Support

Enables the virtual teaching assistant to utilize and respond to diverse learning materials such as PDFs, videos, and historical Piazza posts, fostering a rich, adaptive learning environment.

Knowledge Base for Diverse File Types

Our knowledge base supports documents from multiple sources, enabling it to learn from Piazza posts, videos, and PDFs. Users have the flexibility to view and update this dynamic repository at any time, ensuring it remains a current and valuable educational resource.

Streamlined Piazza Engagement

Our platform monitors Piazza posts in real-time, instantly responding to student questions by leveraging the extensive knowledge base. This ensures students receive accurate and timely assistance, significantly reducing wait times.

  • Seamless Piazza integration
  • Response in 30 seconds, cutting 90% of waiting time
  • 24/7 Support
